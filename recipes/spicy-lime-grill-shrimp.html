Spicy Lime Grilled Shrimp

Description

Spicy Lime Grilled Shrimp packs a bold punch of flavor in every juicy bite. Marinated in a tangy blend of fresh lime juice, garlic, chili, and spices, these shrimp are grilled to perfection â€” smoky, slightly charred, and bursting with heat and citrus. Perfect for cookouts, tacos, salads, or served on skewers as a fiery appetizer, this dish is a vibrant crowd-pleaser that brings the heat with a refreshing twist.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons Cajun Seasoning
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound peeled and deveined medium shrimp (30-40 per pound)

Steps to follow:

  1. Mix together Cajun seasoning, lime juice, and oil in a resealable plastic bag.
  2. Add shrimp, coat with marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator for 20 minutes.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Remove shrimp from marinade; shake off excess. Discard remaining marinade.
  5. Cook shrimp on the preheated grill until bright pink on the outside and the meat is no longer transparent in the center, about 2 minutes per side.
  6. Serve on a plate and enjoy with your favourite starch or carbs.
